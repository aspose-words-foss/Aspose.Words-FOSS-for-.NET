Aspose.Words.RW.Factories namespace presents some factories to use outside of the Aspose.Words.RW.
Aspose.Words.RW.Factories is an intermediate layer between Aspose.Words[.Model] and Aspose.Words.RW.
Aspose.Words[.Model] can depend on Aspose.Words.RW.Factories namespace but not on readers and writers from Aspose.Words.RW.
Please avoid using of Aspose.Words.RW reader and writer classes directly outside of Aspose.Words.RW.
