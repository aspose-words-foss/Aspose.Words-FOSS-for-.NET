Aspose.Common namespace.

These are commonly used utility classes, but they are not very low level. So this is the "2nd level of utility classes".
These classes are widely used, but they require some other lower level packages such as Aspose.IO, Aspose.Crypto etc.

Maybe we can give this namespace a better name or pull these classes into more specific/appropriate groups or namespaces. But at the moment this is good enough.

Utility classes that don't depend on other classes should be placed into the Base folder and the Aspose namespace.
